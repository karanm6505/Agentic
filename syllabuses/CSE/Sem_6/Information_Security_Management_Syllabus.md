### Course Description:
This course provides a comprehensive overview of information security management principles and practices. Students will learn how to develop and implement information security policies, conduct risk assessments, and ensure compliance with relevant regulations and standards. The course emphasizes a practical approach through case studies and real-world examples, preparing students to effectively manage information security risks in organizational settings.

### Prerequisites:
Introduction to Information Technology, Basic Networking Concepts

### Learning Objectives:
1.  Analyze information security risks and vulnerabilities within an organization.
2.  Develop and implement effective information security policies and procedures.
3.  Evaluate different risk management frameworks and methodologies.
4.  Apply compliance standards and regulations to information security practices.
5.  Assess the effectiveness of security controls and incident response plans.
6.  Investigate case studies of security breaches and recommend preventive measures.

### Required Textbooks:
- Whitman, M.E., & Mattord, H.J. *Principles of Information Security*. Cengage Learning, 2020. (ISBN: 978-0357035682)

### Recommended Readings:
-  "NIST Cybersecurity Framework", National Institute of Standards and Technology, 2018.
-  "ISO 27001:2013 - Information security management systems", International Organization for Standardization.
